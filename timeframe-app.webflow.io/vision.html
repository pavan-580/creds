VISION

Our vision is to create the diary of the future.

Every single moment is a Slot. It is the collection of Slots that forms our very existence. But no Slot ever returns.
Wouldn’t it be wonderful, if we could access the moments of the people we love? If past, present and future experiences of the people we care about were easily accessible?
Our vision is to collect all of this world‘s time and fashion it into the diary of the future - a place of memories and dreams, where our stories are immortalised.

Jonathan Davis (Founder, CEO)
